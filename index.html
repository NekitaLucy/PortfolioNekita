My Work

Power-Up

Power-Up, the user-friendly habit tracker app, incorporates a unique feature offering three distinct productivity levels. This design allows users to seamlessly balance their to-do lists and add new habits, fostering a balanced and productive lifestyle.

Using mood boards to brainstorm ideas on the design and testing user preferences between two main styles helped me hone in on my current design for the productivity app.

Skin Remedy

Skin Remedy, a skincare app, was designed to focus on ingredient compatibility. Through in-depth research and analysis, the app provides users personalized recommendations, helping them navigate the complex world of skincare ingredients and find the perfect remedies for their skin.

Prototype testing the high-fidelity mobile frames with five testers gave me helpful feedback to improve the design and functionality of the app..
